Applied Computer Science (ACS) Degree at Dominican University

🎓 In-Person with Remote Option | Open to International Students 🌍

The Applied Computer Science (ACS) program at Dominican University of California offers an in-person learning experience with a remote option, ensuring flexibility for all students, including international applicants. Our program is designed to equip students with the latest in-demand tech skills while providing hands-on experience and direct industry connections.
